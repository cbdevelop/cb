Career at Caterbinge

We are Hiring

At Caterbinge we are shaping the future of the food catering industry using technology. Here, we are bridging the gap between chefs, suppliers, wholesalers and event managers. We are an on-demand food catering service, an aggregator model for chefs and a marketplace for event management individuals. We provide superior quality food by allocating best-experienced chefs around the city to master the taste of every single dish of your custom built menu, not just that we provide delicious cuisines but also assures high-quality service. We claim to reduce at least 25% costs of the end customers and an increase in income source of our partners by 30% as we eradicate the involvement of caterers/ agents. The cutting-edge technology involves unique algorithm proceedings to achieve prompt and accurate pricing along with the smart quantitative analysis of the users placed orders which avoids food wastage and false quotes from the chefs/ agents.
